Common types of bikes

1. Cruisers: These bikes feature a low-slung profile, relaxed riding position, and vintage styling.
2. Touring bikes: Built for long-distance travel, these motorcycles are heavy and have large engines and features for comfort, like large windshields and luggage storage.
3. Sportbikes: Designed for speed and agility, sportbikes are lightweight and have a performance-oriented riding position.
4. Adventure bikes: These are versatile and rugged, designed for both on- and off-road exploration.
5. Naked bikes: These are stripped-down bikes with minimal bodywork and exposed engines.
6. Scooters and mopeds: Small, lightweight, and easy to operate, these are ideal for city commuting.
